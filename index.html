Free Education for all?

Remedy to Society’s Ills

Our society is more divided by wealth than by any other criteria. There is the unbelievably rich few, the very rich, the middle income group, the poor.

Improved quality of life

Free education would lead to more educated people. More educated people in the society leads to overall improvement in the quality of life in the society.

Equal opportunities

Intelligence and talent are not the forte of the wealthy alone. There is lot of untapped and undiscovered talent and intelligence lying covered under impoverishment and destitution.

Secured life

Education is the key to a secured life. As such, it is easier for a student to be successful in future if he is educated as opposed to when he is not educated

Promotes fairness

Free education plays a role in promoting fairness in school and in the society. This is because all children will be guaranteed access to the same level of education.

Better outlook at life

Education empowers children to think positively and have a more informed outlook about life. Free education guarantees that all children will have the same outlook about life.
